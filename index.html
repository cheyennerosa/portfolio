About

I am a web designer and graphic artist for Imagination International, Inc. (III) in Eugene, OR. Imagination International, Inc, is the North American distributor of Copic, a popular marker company from Japan.

I love Clif bars, rock climbing (status:V2), running in the rain, cheesy movies, cooking at home, Sweet Cheeks wine, sunglasses weather, and macro photography.

Please refer to Rosa Design Co. for web design, consulting, and grahic art services. Check back in July 2015 for the launch of rosadesign.co.

Alma Foods

During my time as an undergraduate attending the University of Oregon, I worked as a designer for Allen Hall Advertising (AHA). My first client of 2013 was Robin Wood -- owner of Alma Foods L.L.C. Robin was a delight to work with and believed in my work enough to put it on the label of her paste, dip, and salsa line.

  • Client: Alma Foods L.L.C.
  • Date: May 2013

Blue Dog Mead

The illustration above is an example of the marketing and branding material that I produced while working for Blue Dog Mead (I did not design their logo—it was already created when I began working for them). I accepted a full-time, unpaid internship with Blue Dog Mead as a graphic designer for the summer of 2012. I experienced a great deal of growth while working for Simon Blatz and Simon Spencer. It was encouraging to see my work take form in the real world as posters, stickers, sweatshirts, event signage, website illustrations, etc. My internship with Blue Dog Mead greatly impacted my desire to pursue graphic design.

  • Client: Blue Dog Mead
  • Date: Summer 2013
  • View: Mead

Pikachu in Stop-Motion



During my studies as an advertising student at the University of Oregon, I attended several digital arts classes. One of these classes focused on video production. Our first video project was to create a stop-motion film. I pieced together 100's of photos and screenshots for Pikachu in Stop-Motion. I chose Pikachu as a subject because I loved Pokémon growing up. I mixed the audio myself using royalty free samples and Pikachu sound bits.

  • Class: ARTD 251
  • Date: Winter 2012
  • View: YouTube Channel
